التمرين الأول: القراءة والاستيعاب

النص: Global warming is one of the most pressing issues facing our world today. It is caused by greenhouse gases such as carbon dioxide and methane, which trap heat in the atmosphere. Scientists warn that without immediate action, the consequences could be catastrophic.

السؤال: What are the main causes of global warming mentioned?

الإجابة:

The main causes are greenhouse gases such as carbon dioxide and methane, which trap heat in the atmosphere.

التمرين الثاني: الكتابة الرسمية

السؤال: اكتب رسالة رسمية إلى مدير المدرسة لطلب يوم عطلة بسبب مرض

نموذج إجابة:

Dear Mr./Mrs. [Principal's Name],

I am writing to request a day off from school due to illness. I was not feeling well today and believe it is best for me to rest at home to recover fully.

I will make sure to catch up on any missed work.

Thank you for your understanding.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]

التمرين الثالث: قواعس النحو المتقدمة

السؤال: أكمل الجملة بالشكل الصحيح:

1. If I _______ (have) known about the exam, I _______ (study) harder.

2. The book _______ (write) by this author is very popular.

الإجابة:

1. If I had known about the exam, I would have studied harder. (Third conditional)

2. The book written by this author is very popular. (Past participle as adjective)
