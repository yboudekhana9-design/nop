محمد بلوزداد - موقع الملخصات والتمارين

التمرين الأول: الماضي التام (Present Perfect)

السؤال: أكمل الجمل بالشكل الصحيح:

1. I _______ (finish) my homework.

2. She _______ (visit) Paris three times.

3. They _______ (not / arrive) yet.

الإجابة:

1. I have finished my homework.

2. She has visited Paris three times.

3. They have not arrived yet.

القاعدة: نستخدم Have / Has + Past Participle

التمرين الثاني: الجملة الشرطية (Conditional Sentences)

السؤال: أكمل الجمل الشرطية:

1. If I _______ (be) you, I _______ (study) harder.

2. If she _______ (come) early, we _______ (go) to the cinema.

الإجابة:

1. If I were you, I would study harder. (النوع الثاني - افتراضي)

2. If she comes early, we will go to the cinema. (النوع الأول - ممكن)

التمرين الثالث: الفهم والاستيعاب (Reading Comprehension)

النص:

Technology has changed our lives dramatically. With the internet, we can connect with people around the world instantly. Students can learn new skills from online courses. Doctors can diagnose diseases remotely. However, we must be careful about the negative effects of technology on our mental health and social relationships.

الأسئلة:

1. How has technology changed our lives?

2. What are two benefits of technology mentioned?

الإجابة:

1. Technology has changed our lives dramatically by enabling instant global communication

2. Two benefits: Students can learn from online courses, Doctors can diagnose diseases remotely
