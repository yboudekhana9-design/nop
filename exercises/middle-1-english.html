🌐 English Language - Present Simple Tense

المستوى: السنة الأولى متوسط

المادة: اللغة الإنجليزية

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Understand Present Simple Tense usage
  • Form affirmative and negative sentences
  • Ask and answer questions
  • Recognize verb conjugations

📖 Grammar Lesson

Present Simple Tense

Affirmative: Subject + Verb (+ s/es) + Rest of sentence

Negative: Subject + do/does + NOT + Verb + Rest of sentence

Question: Do/Does + Subject + Verb + Rest of sentence?

Example: I play football. / He plays football. / Does she play football?

📊 Verb Conjugation Table

الضمير الفعل (Play) النفي
I play do not play
You play do not play
He/She/It plays does not play
We play do not play
They play do not play

❓ Practice Exercises

Exercise 1: Choose the correct form

She _____ to school every day. (go / goes / going)

أ) go    ب) goes    ج) going

✅ الإجابة الصحيحة: ب) goes

مع الضمير (He/She/It) نضيف (-s) أو (-es) للفعل في زمن Present Simple

Exercise 2: Complete the negative sentence

They _____ like pizza. (don't / doesn't / do not)

أ) doesn't    ب) don't    ج) do not like

✅ الإجابة الصحيحة: ب) don't

مع الضمائر (I, You, We, They) نستخدم (don't)، أما مع (He/She/It) نستخدم (doesn't)

Exercise 3: Form a question

_____ you speak English? (Do / Does / Don't)

أ) Do    ب) Does    ج) Don't

✅ الإجابة الصحيحة: أ) Do

لتكوين سؤال في Present Simple، نضع (Do/Does) في بداية الجملة. مع (you) نستخدم (Do)

💡 Tips for Success

  • Remember: He/She/It always takes 's' or 'es'
  • Use 'does' for questions and negatives with He/She/It
  • Use 'do' for I, You, We, They
  • Practice writing sentences every day
  • Read English texts to see the tense in action
